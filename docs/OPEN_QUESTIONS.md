# Open Questions

- Why does verified history become economically valuable in some cases but not others?
- When is a receipt ownable versus merely inspectable?
- Can receipts accumulate reputation over time without collapsing into opaque scoring systems?
- How should portfolios of receipts be structured?
- Can a Chronicle survive after the originating runtime or company disappears?
- How should transfer work without breaking verifiability?
- What is the relationship between off-chain evidence and on-chain ownership claims?
- When should Chronicle reuse ERC/NFT standards and when should it ignore them?
- Do receipts deserve a dedicated standard rather than adaptation into existing token formats?
- What are the legal and archival implications of owning verifiable work history?
