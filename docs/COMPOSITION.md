# Composition

Receipts become more powerful when they can compose.

## Composition targets

Multiple receipts may form:

- a mission history
- a company history
- a research history
- a contributor portfolio
- a machine or model lineage
- a creator or engineering reputation trail

## Why composition matters

A single receipt may prove one event.
A Chronicle can prove a pattern.

That pattern may carry greater value because it reflects:

- sustained execution
- repeated verification
- evolving capability
- historical continuity
- cumulative relevance

## Portfolio logic

Chronicle asks whether receipts can become portfolio primitives.

A portfolio of receipts may represent:

- verified engineering work
- verified scientific work
- verified creative work
- verified operational history

That makes composition central to the asset thesis.
