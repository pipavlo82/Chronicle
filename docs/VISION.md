# Vision

Chronicle explores a future where verifiable receipts of work become durable digital assets.

The central idea is not that a receipt decorates an asset.
The central idea is that the receipt itself may become the asset.

A Chronicle is not a static collectible. It is a growing body of verified history:

- work performed
- evidence produced
- verification state
- relationships between receipts
- ownership and stewardship over time

The long-term vision is a world where individuals, teams, labs, companies, and autonomous systems can hold portable, verifiable histories that survive the platforms and runtimes that originally generated them.

Chronicle asks whether such histories can become as important as:

- source code history
- academic citation history
- scientific provenance
- engineering track records
- research reputation

The key difference is that Chronicle treats those histories as cryptographically grounded digital objects rather than loose institutional records.
